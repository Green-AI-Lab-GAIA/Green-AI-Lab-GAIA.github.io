GAIA - Green AI LAB

About Us

GAIA is an initiative inside the Green AI LAB at FGV. This research division focuses on leveraging Artificial Intelligence to address environmental questions. Our main areas of research include:

  • Forest monitoring using computer vision.
  • Tree species recognition.
  • Detecting climate changes using spatiotemporal data.
